HTML elements are the building blocks of HTML pages. With HTML contracts,images and other objects such as interactive forms may be embedded into the rendered page.HTML provides a means to create strructured documents by denoting structural semantics for text as headings, paragraphs,lists,links,quotes and other items.HTML elements are delineated by tags,written using angle brackets.Tags such as <img/> and <input/> directly introduce content into the page. Other tags such as <p> surround and provide information about documents text and may include other tags as sub-elements.Browsers do not display the HTML tags but use them to interpret the content of the page.
